Core Algorithm

Dynamic Range Prediction Engine

Range = Base_Range × (Battery% / 100) × Weather_Factor × Traffic_Factor

🌡️ Weather Factor

Temp < 10°C → ×0.85
Temp < 0°C → ×0.70
Normal → ×1.00

Cold reduces lithium-ion battery efficiency (proven chemistry)

🚦 Traffic Factor

Heavy → ×0.75
Medium → ×0.90
Light → ×1.00

Stop-and-go traffic = regenerative braking waste

📍 Haversine Formula

Used to calculate real GPS distance between coordinates for battery drain tracking & hub sorting by proximity.

Industry-standard geodesic math

Example: 500km base range, 80% battery, 5°C weather, Heavy traffic → 306 km predicted range (vs naive 400 km — a 24% difference judges can verify!)

Feature Showcase

What Makes It Unique

🔐

Smart Authentication

Indian phone (10-digit), vehicle plate format validation. Login is remembered via localStorage — no re-login needed on refresh.

🚦

Real Traffic Visualization

Route is split into 15 color-coded segments — Green (fast), Yellow (moderate), Red (heavy) — exactly like Google Maps.

🧭

Gyroscope Navigation

Location dot has a directional arrow that rotates with your phone's physical orientation using the DeviceOrientation API.

⚡

Reserve a Port

Tap any hub on the map and click "Reserve Port" — sends a dispatch message with your name, vehicle number & phone to the station.

🚨

Emergency SOS

One-tap floating emergency button that triggers a protocol alert with your live location.

❌

Cancel Trip

Instantly resets the entire drive mode, clears the route, alerts, and hubs — returning cleanly to planning screen.
